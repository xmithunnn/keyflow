Every feature, explained

Built for typing that actually looks human.

Keyflow is not an autoclicker. It is a full typing simulation engine with variable timing, natural errors, and rhythm patterns modelled on real human behaviour.

15 to 300

Custom WPM

Set your exact typing speed anywhere in the 15 to 300 WPM range. Fixed rate or natural drift, your call.

Varies every key

Human rhythm engine

No two keystrokes hit at the same interval. Keyflow adds micro-jitter between every character, slows on harder words, and speeds through familiar ones.

Configurable

Mistake simulation

Types a wrong character, pauses for 80 to 200 ms, backspaces, and corrects itself. Rate is adjustable from 1 to 12 percent.

Always on

Typing drift

Subtle speed variation accumulates across a run. Long paragraphs slow slightly, short sentences fly. Looks exactly like a tired human at 11pm.

Punctuation-aware

Smart pausing

Slows at commas, stops longer at periods and question marks, hesitates before long words. The typing engine reads the structure of your text.

Configurable delay

Paragraph breaks

Adds a longer natural pause between paragraphs to simulate re-reading and thinking before continuing.

Pro feature

Session chunks

Splits your text into multiple fake editing sessions with longer gaps between them, mimicking real writing behaviour over time.

Pro feature

Cursor drift

Occasionally selects a few words back, pauses, then retypes. Looks like the kind of editing a real writer does mid-sentence.

Up to 10 seconds

Start delay

Gives you time to click into your target field before typing starts. Choose 2, 3, 5, or 10 seconds.

Any Chrome input

Platform support

If it is a text box inside Chrome, Keyflow works in it. Google Docs, Notion, Gmail, Canvas, Turnitin, Word Online, all of them.
