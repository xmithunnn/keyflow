How it works

Three steps and you are running

No account needed for the free tier. No complicated setup. Just install, paste, go.

1. Install the extension

Download the zip, unzip it, go to chrome://extensions, enable Developer Mode, and load the folder. Takes under a minute.

2. Paste your text and configure

Open the extension popup, paste your text, and set WPM, mistakes, and drift to match your style.

3. Click into any field and hit Run

Click into Google Docs, a text box, anywhere. Hit Run. Keyflow types your text exactly like you would have.
