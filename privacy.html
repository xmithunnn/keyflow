Privacy Policy

Last updated May 2025

Keyflow collects only what it needs to operate. Here is exactly what that means.

What we collect

  • Email address — taken at checkout via Stripe, used only to send your license key and renewal notices.
  • Device identifier — an anonymous ID generated locally, used only to enforce per-device license limits. Never linked to personal info.
  • Payment data — handled entirely by Stripe. We never see or store card numbers.

What we do not collect

  • The text you type or paste into Keyflow
  • Which websites you visit
  • Anything that could identify you beyond your email

Third-party services

  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Supabase — encrypted license key storage
  • Resend — transactional email delivery

Data retention

Your email and license record are kept while your subscription is active. You can request deletion anytime at hello@keyflow.live.
